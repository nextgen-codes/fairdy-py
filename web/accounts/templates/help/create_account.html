How to create an account on this website.

  1. Go to the registration page.
  2. Choose a username- this will be publicly visible on all simulations you run.
    • must be 150 characters or fewer
    • must be composed of alphanumeric characters, and _ @ + . -
  3. Choose a password- enter twice to verify.
    • must be at least 9 characters long
    • must not be too similar to username or email
    • must not be in a list of the 20,000 most common passwords
    • must not be entirely numeric
  4. Confirm you agree with terms and conditions:
    • This website uses cookies to optimize user experience by storing information about your session.
    • This website will store your username and email, as well as timestamps associated with registration, login, use, etc.
    • The simulations you run will be publicly visible with your username, your email will only be visible to website administrators.
    • You may delete your account at any time by going to your profile and following the 'Delete Account' link.
    • After you delete your account, all simulations you have run will remain in our database, but without any reference to your former account or personal data.
  5. Follow the link in your email to verify your account. If you have not received a link:
    • Check your spam folder
    • Resend the email confirmation from your profile page when logged in
    • Try using another email address, this can be updated on your profile page when logged in
  6. Start simulating storage system life cycles!
    • You can only run simulations when you are logged in.
    • The number of simulations you can run within a 30 day period is limited, check your profile page for the current status.
