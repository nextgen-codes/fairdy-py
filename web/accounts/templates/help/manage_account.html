Remaining Credits

While FaiRDy is a lightweight framework for characterizing the reliability of storage systems over their lifecycle, it still requires a certain amount of compute and memory to process the simulations. For this reason and because the server is hosted on hardware at the University of Stavanger, new accounts have a Block Cycle Limit, which limits the amount of simulations within a month. Block Cycle Limit is calculated as the size of the storage system (in blocks) multiplied by the number of cycles. New accounts have a default Block Cycle Limit of 500 million, your remaining credits are shown with a progress bar on your profile page. Considering that most systems reveal few additional insights after 100 cycles, the default limit allows you to run 100 simulations with up to 50,000 total blocks in each system. This should be sufficient for most cases to casually explore the effects of erasure code parameters on storage system life cycles, but please contact us if you would like to request an increase in your limit.

Email Validation

You will not be able to run simulation until you verify your email address by clicking a link we email to you. If you have not received a validation email, check your spam folder, or use the links on your profile page to send a new confirmation or change your email address. Note that changing your email will require you validate the new email address before running any simulations.

Delete Account

If you wish to delete your FaiRDy account we will remove all of your personal information from our database. Either follow the link in your profile page, or click here. The simulations you have ran with your account will remain in our database, but they will no longer be associated with your former username. You must enter your password to delete your account, if you have forgotten your password, first reset your password here, then follow the delete link from your profile page after validating your email.

Forgot Password

If you have forgotten your password, go to the forgot password page and enter your username. You will receive a validation email, and upon following the link it contains you will be able to reset the password for your account.
